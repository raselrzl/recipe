How To Make Very Good Burger

Berger is really dilicious having meat and vegetables with best souces. Since burger contain both meat and vegitables,As a human this is one of the best and easy solution to have in our daily routine.

ingredients

  • Meat
  • Sallad
  • Bread
  • Dressing
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Paper

Complete Process

  1. Firstly, Prepare the sauce or dressing, We gonna prepare chilli sauce. For that, Take some myannas, chilli, jalapeno, salt and black paper, Mixed all this together.
  2. Prepare vegitables, We can put any kind of vegitables whatever we want. But I suggest Sallad, Tomat and Onion because this is perfect with chilli burger
  3. Now Meat, I hope we have meat that is already formed for burger. We make burger patty and put on the steak (200 degree temperature) for 8 minutes, salt and blackpaper on the top of the meat, after 4 minutes turn the meat on the other side
  4. Then we put bread on the steak for making warm for 45second-1minute
  5. Finnally, we take a dish, first put bottom side of bread then dressing, sallad, tomat, meat, onion some sauce agin and than top side of the bread sequentially
  6. Opps, One step left, We put cheese on the top of the meat during cooking just because cheese should be melth.
  7. Enjoy!
